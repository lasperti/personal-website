Lucas Asperti.

Undergraduate Computer Science student at the Institute of Mathematics, Statistics and Computer Science of the University of São Paulo. Challenged by its Theoretical Computer Science track.

Seeking answers in binary exploitation, formal methods, philosophy and the machinery in between.

Operating on both sides of the architecture at IME-USP. Maintaining the GNU/Linux Network, and tearing systems apart as President of IMEsec.

Searching for the sublime in art during the off-time. Foundational media includes Mother 3 (2006), Demonlover (2002), The Sopranos (1999-2007), Morangos Mofados (1982), and Lift Your Skinny Fists Like Antennas To Heaven (2000).
